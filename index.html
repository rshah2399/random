How to use the wheel spinner

It's easy: type in your entries in the textbox to the right of the wheel, then click the wheel to spin it and get a random winner.

Can the wheel be rigged?

There is no functionality to determine which entry will win ahead of time. When you click the wheel, it accelerates for a random time and then decelerates to a stop.

We are proud to share that 100% of the electricity that powers our servers is renewable, and 93% comes from carbon-free energy sources.
